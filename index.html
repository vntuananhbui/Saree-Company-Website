ABOUT US

We're Saree

Saigon Refrigeration Engineering Co., Ltd is a leading provider of industrial refrigeration solutions. We specialize in design consultancy, equipment supply, manufacturing, installation, and maintenance of refrigeration equipment for various industries including marine processing, agriculture, and cold storage. With our experienced team of engineers and technicians, we have successfully completed numerous large-scale projects nationwide. Our expertise and dedication ensure that we meet the specific needs of our customers in the most efficient and reliable manner.
